Finder Heart Award Concepts

Exploring the structural morph between the Finder Eyes and the Heart.

Concept 1: The Inner Core

A crystal-clear acrylic heart. The blue Finder eyes are suspended inside as a glowing inner core, radiating the green/orange/red gradient outward. Massive logo etched on the flat front face.

Concept 2: The Structural Morph

The base is the flat bottom of the eye panel. As it rises, it physically splits into the two rounded arches of the heart. The massive logo forms the structural base.

Concept 3: The Negative Space

Two separate sculptural pillars (representing the eyes) stand side-by-side. The negative space between them forms the perfect shape of a heart.

Concept 4: The Geometric Interlock

A dual-layer award. A solid blue Finder eye panel sits behind a translucent gradient heart. They interlock physically, bridged by a massive metal 'finder' logo.

Concept 5: The Monolith

A sleek, monolithic shield shape combining the flat bottom of the eyes and the arches of the heart. Gradient only on the edges catching the light, with a massive logo dominating the clean front.
